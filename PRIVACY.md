# **Privacy Policy**

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024

## **1\. Introduction**

Thank you for using **Image Tagger** ("the Application"). This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled. We believe in total privacy: your data belongs to you, and it should stay on your device.

## **2\. Data Collection & Usage**

**The Application operates 100% locally (offline).**

* **No Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or transmit your name, email, IP address, or location.  
* **No Usage Tracking:** We do not track how you use the app, which buttons you click, or how long you use it.  
* **No Analytics:** We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, or any other third-party tracking software.

## **3\. File Access Permissions**

To function, the Application requires permission to access the specific files and folders you select on your computer's hard drive.

* **Read Access:** Required to display your images and read your text/CSV input files.  
* **Write Access:** Required to embed metadata tags (EXIF) into your image files or to save cropped versions of images.  
* **Scope:** The Application only modifies files in the specific folders you choose (via the "Browse" buttons). It does not scan your background files or other directories.

## **4\. Internet Connectivity**

The Application does not require an internet connection to function. It does not send any data to external servers.

## **5\. Changes to This Policy**

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to comply with Microsoft Store guidelines or new feature requirements. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## **6\. Contact Us**

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us via the [GitHub Issues page](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://github.com/YourUsername/ImageTagger/issues).
